## Instructions

To navigate through the `Presentations`, you can use:
- Top or bottom bar; 
- The arrow keys (i.e. &uarr; and &darr;);
- The keyboard shortcuts `j` and `k`, respectively up and down.

Also:

- `gg`: go to presentation's start.
- `G`: go to presentation's end.
